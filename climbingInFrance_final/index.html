Early Summer Climbing in France

Early summer time is the best time for me climbing in the south east of France. Particularly the day gets longer so you have a longer outdoor time to climb, and the weather is pleasant and less crowded than summer time. Ceuse is a well-known world-class sport climbing area. Even though there are many professional and amateur climbers from many countries climbing in Ceuse, it is still full of a friendly atmosphere.

My favorite climbing area is Saint Leger because there are lovely people living in the small village near the crag in Saint Leger, and we became friends by the end of my stay.
